Biography

Quinn Fieldstone was born and raised in Calgary, Alberta spending all of her free time training to be a professional dancer. At the age of 18 she received her first professional contract with Ballet Ouest as a soloist for 2 years. She then signed a contract with The Virginia National Ballet and by her fourth season with the company, was promoted to Soloist and Assistant Ballet Master. After a 7 year professional career her injuries were beyond recovery. Ms. Fieldstone made a drastic career shift and began diving into computer programming and coding. By July 2023 she will graduate from MITxpro: Professional Certificate in Coding. Both of these professions are pattern based so the shift has been smooth. Ms. Fieldstone is hard working, a quick learner, and a driven individual who is excited to begin working in the industry. Email: Quinnfieldstone98@gmail.com
